How to Play

Objective: Catch as many snowflakes as possible within the time limit.

Start the Game: Click the "Start Game" button to begin.

Catch the Snowflakes: Click on the snowflake as quickly as possible.

Level Up: Score 5 points to advance to the next level. The timer resets to 30 seconds and the game becomes more challenging.

Time Limit: You have 30 seconds at each level. If you don't catch a snowflake within 1.5 seconds, it will disappear and spawn in a new location.

Scoring: Each snowflake caught gives you points equal to your current level.

Game Over: The game ends when the timer reaches zero. Your final score will be displayed.
